Operation Bunyan-un-Marsous – 10 May 2025
On 10 May 2025, Pakistan launched Operation Bunyan-un-Marsous in response to Indian aggression. Named after a Qur’anic phrase meaning “a solid, unbreakable wall,” the operation featured precise missile, drone, and cyber strikes on key military targets. Pakistan’s Air Force shot down six Indian jets, including three Rafale, one MiG-29, and two Sukhoi Su-30. The swift, coordinated action demonstrated Pakistan’s unity, strength, and resolve to defend its sovereignty.
